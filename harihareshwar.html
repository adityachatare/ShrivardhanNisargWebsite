Harihareshwar
The last point of Raigad district is Harihareshwar. Harihareshwar is also known as 'Dakshin Kaashi'. As this is place of worship and also have beach so Maharashtra Govenment declared it as a tourist place. To reach Harihareshwar you have to take right from mangaon 33kms inside on Mumbai - Goa Highway.
Shree Harihareshwar Mandir
Shree Somjai Mandir

Somjai Devi is popularly called as 'Gram Devat' of Shriwardhankars. As we turn right side from shivaji chowk on tilak road exactly on right side 'Shree Somjai Mandir' is there. It has big ground and main mandir is in pure kokani style with slopping roofs.

This Temple is on of the ancient Structure in Kokan. Its is belived that 'Agasti Muni' had setuped Somjai. In the month of December after Diwali, 'Rath Utsav' takes place here. Next day 'Maha Prasad' is given to everyone.
Peshave Mandir



Peshave Mandir is just 5 minutes distance from Somjai Mandir. As we go ahead from Somjai Mandir, by taking first left we enter in Peshave aali and in that opposite to the road Peshave Mandir is there. Peshave mandir is one of the Ancient Historical Structure in Kokan. Its was the house of 'SHRIMANT BALAJI VISHVANATHRAO PESHAVE'. Also he took birth at this place. Its like a big fort surrounded by wall which contains bricks of ancient times. One statue of 'Balaji Vishvanathrao Peshave' is placed at right corner.
Jeevna Bundar (Port)

Once we reach at beach, looking right side will find a huge mountain, below the mountain ‘Port’ is settled, commonly called as ‘Jeevna Bundar’. Here is we will find the area of ‘Koli’ peoples (Fisherman Families). In this area we will find the transportation of Sea foods, Packaging of Sea foods, Fishing nets etc. On this mountain one ‘Light House’ is there which proves Informer for Fishermans.

In previous days, Waterways transportation use to take place form shriwardhan port. Mumbai to Dabhol and Dabhol to Mumbai was the route on this port. At 8 to 8.30 PM passengers use to come at shriwardhan port. At late night i.e 12.00 PM the boat use to come at shriwardhan, but it was standing in deep sea as the port was too small. Then a boat frm port was use to pick up the passengers from port, and drop till the boat in deep sea. On this route, 2 boats was famous named ‘Jayanti’ & ‘Tukaram’. During the year 1940-50 they got an accident and near Harihareshwar.
Shree Kusumadevi Mandir

Kusumadevi Mandir is a bit different from shriwardhan as it comes outside from shriwardhan inside hills & mountains in full of green area. In Navratri’s and Chaitra gauri pujan, a big fair takes place here. It says that this place is from ‘Pandavas’ times. So this place had got an historical importance. Also it got a green nature, its a gods huge gift to this place.
