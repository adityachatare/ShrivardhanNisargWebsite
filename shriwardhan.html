Shriwardhan

'Shriwardhan' The name itselfis giving the information of about richness of town. Totally surrounded by coconut & arecanut trees, silent nature, fresh environment & filly green in Monsoon is the quality of SHRIWARDHAN. The town is small but its a very beautiful place for holidays. Sloping roofed houses protected by the shadow of coconut trees is like we are in the hands of our mother nature. Many people staying in Metropolitian cities like Mumbai, Pune always becames bor of their daily routines & schedules. For them shriwardhan is the best place to became free from daily life. Shriwardhan always welcomes this kind of people.
Shriwardhan Beach

Tilak road & Market road from town directly brings us at Beach. Extremely Silent, Cleaned and Safe beach of Shriwardhan is the most attractive point of Kokan Tourism.
Shree Somjai Mandir

Somjai Devi is popularly called as 'Gram Devat' of Shriwardhankars. As we turn right side from shivaji chowk on tilak road exactly on right side 'Shree Somjai Mandir' is there. It has big ground and main mandir is in pure kokani style with slopping roofs.

This Temple is on of the ancient Structure in Kokan. Its is belived that 'Agasti Muni' had setuped Somjai. In the month of December after Diwali, 'Rath Utsav' takes place here. Next day 'Maha Prasad' is given to everyone.
Shree Peshave Mandir

Peshave Mandir is just 5 minutes distance from Somjai Mandir. As we go ahead from Somjai Mandir, by taking first left we enter in Peshave aali and in that opposite to the road Peshave Mandir is there. Peshave mandir is one of the Ancient Historical Structure in Kokan. Its was the house of 'SHRIMANT BALAJI VISHVANATHRAO PESHAVE'. Also he took birth at this place. Its like a big fort surrounded by wall which contains bricks of ancient times. One statue of 'Balaji Vishvanathrao Peshave' is placed at right corner.
